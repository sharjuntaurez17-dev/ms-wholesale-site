Since 1965

M Shaul Hammed Rowther Sons

For over six decades, M Shaul Hammed Rowther Sons has been a trusted name in wholesale rice across Coimbatore and Tamil Nadu. From our base at Town Hall, we supply premium boiled, idli, basmati, raw, biryani and Kerala matta rice to retailers, hotels and households.

Our commitment to quality, fair pricing and consistent supply has earned the loyalty of thousands of customers and more than 100 supplier partners. Every grain we deliver carries the trust built since 1965.
